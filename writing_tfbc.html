The Frostburn Chronicles


 

Enter Fireteam Two-Six-Five: better known as Phoenix Family, the fireteam of orphans. Titus Berguard is joined by his companions Valentina, Sylvia, and Romin as a cadet in the Human academies on Fosroth, fighting to obtain the highest rank of Dragon class that only five fireteams are fierce enough to claim out of a hundred.

The cadets train in anticipation of fighting the Chymaera beyond the walls, sworn enemies for thousands of years harnessing the powers of death, and the reason why the Humans lost their capital city and the powers of fire and life. Without a Chosen or a Guardian, they are on the verge of liquidation, with no hopes to reclaim the essence and bring humanity to a second golden age. But with his avid curiosity, Titus prematurely uncovers truths of his orphaned past, of his destiny set in motion by forces nearly a century before. Undiscovered connections to his fireteam prove their connections to run deeper than their blood spilled on the Academy training fields, and with circum- stances leaving them far beyond the city walls with more problems than just the salvation of the Humans, they must carry out the work of the heroes a decade before.
